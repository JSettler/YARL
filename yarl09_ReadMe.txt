
 keyboard commands for YARL v0.9:
 ---------------------------------

 <cursor keys> (up, down, left, right): move your character (shown as blue disc)

 'u','d': go upstairs/downstairs (when standing on the stairs tile)

 'a': attack weakest adjacent monster

 quit game: <escape>    (automatically saves your game to disk)

 when starting, you have the option to load a savegame (if exists)
